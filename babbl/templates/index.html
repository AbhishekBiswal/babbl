About Babbl

Babbl is a group-based network where people can post about anything anonymously. Technically, we don't ask any user for their identity, in any form.

We don't plan to host any funny comments about someone, or any embarassing stuff. Still reading this? Oh well, go ahead, we don't give a shit about what you post.
